What TARS does.

Six scheduled tasks covering the full weekly strategy cycle — from session briefings to action tracking. Click any card to see details.

Every Friday · 4:39 PM
Friday Meeting Briefing
Processes the Fireflies transcript from your weekly strategy session and delivers a full executive briefing — decisions, actions, themes, watch list, and a strategic narrative — within minutes of the session ending.
Delivers
💬 #strategy-feed post 📧 Gmail briefing draft 📁 Drive session folder 📄 Briefing doc + JSON archive
What it produces
  • Key Highlights — 3–5 sentence strategic narrative
  • Key Decisions made in the session
  • All actions with owners and due dates
  • Emerging themes across sessions
  • Watch list — risks and gaps with owners
How it stores data
  • Creates a dated folder in the Strategic Sessions Drive
  • Saves briefing as a Google Doc inside that folder
  • Saves a structured JSON archive for machine-readable recall
  • Writes session memory to #cowork-memory for downstream tasks
Every Thursday · 4:04 PM
Thursday Meeting Preview
The pre-read for tomorrow's strategy session. Surfaces unresolved tensions, open actions, patterns from past sessions, and the questions worth putting on the agenda — so Friday's conversation starts with context.
Delivers
💬 #strategy-feed post 📋 Agenda suggestions
What it covers
  • Open actions from previous sessions
  • Unresolved decisions and tensions
  • Watch list items requiring attention
  • Patterns and themes across the last 3–4 sessions
  • Suggested agenda priorities for Friday
Every Monday · 10:06 AM
Monday Action Reminder
Kicks off the week by DM-ing each person their open actions — connected to the bigger strategic picture, not just a task list. Also creates a weekly team email draft for Felipe with curated key actions grouped by owner.
Delivers
💬 Personal DMs to each owner 📧 Weekly actions email draft
How it works
  • Reads open actions from the Action Tracker and session memory
  • DMs each person their specific actions with strategic context
  • Flags anything overdue or at risk
  • Creates a Gmail draft to the full team with prioritised weekly actions
  • Links to the full tracker in Google Drive
Mon–Fri · 5:07 PM
Daily Completion Check
Reads #strategy-feed every day at 5 PM looking for completion signals. When someone marks something done, TARS confirms it, updates the tracker immediately, and stores it in memory for Friday's full sync.
Delivers
✅ DM confirmation to owner 📊 Tracker updated same day 💬 #strategy-feed acknowledgement
Completion keywords it detects
  • done, completed, finished, closed, delivered
  • sorted, wrapped up, shipped, sent, confirmed, resolved
What happens next
  • Matched to an action ID → confirmation DM sent to you
  • Action Tracker xlsx updated in Google Drive immediately
  • Completion stored in memory for Friday's tracker sync
  • If ambiguous → TARS DMs you asking which action you mean
Every Friday · 7:05 PM
Weekly Tracker Update
After the Friday briefing, TARS does a full reconciliation of the Action Tracker — syncing all completions captured during the week, updating statuses, and flagging anything overdue that didn't move.
Delivers
📊 Action_Tracker.xlsx updated 📁 Drive sync complete
What it updates
  • Pulls completions from weekly memory (daily-completion-check)
  • Updates Action_Tracker.xlsx with new statuses and dates
  • Adds newly created actions from the Friday session
  • Flags overdue items for Monday's reminder
Every Monday · 9:06 AM
Drive Session Audit
Audits the Strategic Sessions folder in Google Drive every Monday morning, checking that session folders follow the correct naming convention and that no sessions are missing archives or briefing docs.
Delivers
🔍 Drive audit report ⚠️ Naming violations flagged
What it checks
  • Folder names match: YYYY-MM-DD - Session NN
  • Each session has a briefing doc
  • Each session has a JSON archive
  • Recurring violations tracked in memory

How to use #strategy-feed.

You don't need commands or special syntax. Just post naturally — TARS reads the channel every day at 5 PM and figures out the rest.

✅ Marking an action complete

Post naturally in the channel. TARS recognises completion language and matches it to your open actions.

Billing integration is done ✅
→ TARS matches to your open action and confirms via DM
Finished the Google/Apple compliance review
→ TARS sends confirmation + updates the tracker
Sorted — contract sent to Teya
→ Matched, confirmed, tracker updated same day

Keywords TARS listens for: done · completed · finished · closed · delivered · sorted · wrapped up · shipped · sent · confirmed · resolved · handled

💬 What TARS does next
1
Clear match found — TARS DMs you: "✅ Got it Simon — S08-A03 marked complete. Nice work."
2
Tracker updated — Action_Tracker.xlsx is updated in Google Drive that same evening.
3
Channel acknowledgement — TARS posts once to #strategy-feed if completions were found.
4
If unclear — TARS DMs you asking which action you mean. No guessing, no flagging to others.
📌 Posting context or updates

Use #strategy-feed to share blockers, progress, or context mid-week. TARS reads all of it and surfaces relevant updates in Thursday's preview and Friday's briefing.

FYI — Snowflake migration pushed to April
→ Picked up in Thursday preview as a potential blocker

→ Noted as progress in Friday's session briefing
🔒 Privacy & personal comms

Any wellbeing or personal observations are handled privately — TARS sends those directly to Felipe via DM, never in group channels.

#strategy-feed is for business context only. Personal or sensitive matters go via DM with Felipe.

TARS never posts personal or sensitive information into group channels.
